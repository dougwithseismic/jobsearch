## Risk Assessment

- **What could go wrong:**
  1. **Java/Kotlin backend gap is a dealbreaker.** Some companies have strict "must have" stack requirements. Tractive's job description lists Java/Kotlin + Spring Boot as required skills.
  2. **Salary ceiling at EUR 65k is non-negotiable.** The Senior SE Full-Stack role explicitly posts EUR 65k/year. Even with the 4-day work week, this is below Doug's market rate. If Tractive won't negotiate, this becomes a quality-of-life vs. compensation tradeoff.
  3. **Linz commute is impractical.** 3 hours from Prague, hybrid 2 days remote. This means 2-3 days/week in Linz. Depending on Doug's personal situation, this could be a dealbreaker.
  4. **Role already filled.** No posted closing date, but with 17+ open positions, Tractive is clearly scaling. The Senior Full-Stack Developer role could fill quickly.
  5. **Limited career growth.** Glassdoor reviews mention limited advancement potential. With 4 co-founders in leadership and 270+ employees, there may be a ceiling.

- **Mitigation:**
  1. **Backend gap:** Lead with React/frontend strength and frame backend adaptability as a positive: "I've worked across Node, Python, and can pick up JVM languages. In my experience, full-stack IoT work is frontend-heavy, and React is my strongest language." Offer to do a technical challenge or take-home project to prove capability.
  2. **Salary:** Negotiate by emphasizing the business value Doug brings: "I don't just build features, I move subscription metrics. At Patrianna, I led the growth team that directly contributed to 0-to-1M user scaling. I'm looking for EUR [X] given my experience level, but I'm open to discussing a performance-based compensation structure or equity if Tractive is willing." If EUR 65k is truly the ceiling, decide whether the 4-day work week + quality of life compensates.
  3. **Commute:** Discuss remote flexibility during the interview process: "I'm based in Prague, 3 hours from Linz. I'm happy to be in the office 2-3 days/week, but would appreciate flexibility to work remotely the other days. I've successfully managed distributed collaboration at multiple companies." If Tractive is strict on in-office requirements, this may not be a fit.
  4. **Timing:** Apply ASAP. If the role fills before Doug applies, revisit in 3-6 months when Tractive posts another full-stack opening (given their growth trajectory, this is likely).
  5. **Career growth:** Decide whether career growth matters more than quality of life. Tractive may not offer a fast path to VP/Director roles, but the 4-day work week, 4.8 Glassdoor rating, and fun product make it a strong lifestyle choice.
